Dawn of War has races. And so do we. Except everyone is the same race in R2K12SOW, so we call them factions. In the multifaceted warfare of the Risen universe, it's not as clear cut as nation versus nation. No, countries, empires, multinational organizations, corporations, and cults all have a part in the conflict. Unfortunately we can't put them all in, even five of them may be too much. Hell, some of the most well manned DOW mods only strive to put one race in. But is that the reason why you're here? No! You're here to look at some pretty logos and find out some more information. So let's go!
